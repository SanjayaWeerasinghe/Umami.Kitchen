Baby gem lettuce

Hydroponic. No pesticides. Torn — never chopped — so the leaves keep their structure through delivery.

02

Heirloom tomato

Redfield Growers · Hudson Valley

Six varieties, whichever is peak that week. Quartered, salted two minutes, drained.

03

Wild shrimp

Gulf Wild · Certified sustainable

Sous-vide at 63°C for exactly four minutes. Never frozen after cooking. Twelve grams protein per bowl.

04

English cucumber

Longview Greenhouse · New Jersey

Grown in glass, cut to 3mm on a Japanese mandoline. Held on ice until assembly.

Yamada Brewery · Wakayama Prefecture

Fermented in cedar barrels for 24 months. Deep, malty, gentle on salt. We pour twelve millilitres.

Half black, half golden. Dry-toasted in a cast-iron pan until they just start to jump.

Any time before 11pm the night before. From the app, one tap. No emailing "please pause."
